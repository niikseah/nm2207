What is your project about? It analyses popularity of animes based on various factors like: number of episodes, length of episodes, studio which produced the anime, genre of anime. It doubles as a website that recommends anime to users, where they are able to search for animes similar to the ones they enjoy watching.
What is the data you plan to use? https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gianinamariapetrascu/top-250-anime-2023. It includes data on the top anime in 2023 and categorises these animes.
What is the question you plan to answer? What do viewers like to watch?
Why is this an important question? This analysis can be useful for animation studios and writers to decide what stories to write and what animes to adapt and produce. The website can also be used by viewers to find animes similar to the ones they enjoy watching.
Which rows and columns of the dataset do you plan to use, to answer this question? Rank,Title,Popularity,Genre,Studio,Type,Episodes,Duration,Start_date,End_date,Members,Score
Who is your audience People are planning to watch anime / Anime studios / Writers
What is at stake - benefits Ease in finding anime to watch. Data presented shows what makes an anime popular more clearly.
